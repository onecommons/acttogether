How it works

Anyone can give a penny a day.

We’re creating a new way for people to pool small amounts together and decide which projects should be funded. Give a penny, dime, or dollar a day.

A small contribution can make a big impact.

Every month, we do funding rounds where the most endorsed projects get financial support. When we all come together to choose, we can make a big impact with our individual contributions.

Get involved at any level you want.

  • Endorse projects (endorse as many as you want)
  • Ask questions to make sure projects are well thought-through
  • Add information and advice
  • Spread the word

From local to global

Whether you are passionate about solving a local problem in your community or you want global change, you can use the OneCommons platform to come together with others to make a difference.

We’re making participation easy

Once you’re a member, it’s as simple as clicking a button to endorse a project. You can also get more involved. Instead of writing a check to an organization and that’s, you get to participate at the level that you want and stay involved.

The nitty-gritty

Join OneCommons.

Join for as little as a penny a day. Make a fixed monthly donation. You can cancel at any time.

Choose which projects and organizations to endorse.

Since you’re not donating a specific amount of money, you can endorse as many projects as you like. You can also endorse another member’s endorsements or endorse a group focused on a local or global issue.

You can also give time, advice, and information, and ask questions to help make sure the projects are well thought-through.

The more you engage and contribute the more your endorsements count. Learn more

Connect with other members

On our own we feel isolated and powerless. Together we can build a community of people who care about making a difference through real, tangible projects that make an impact on the local or global level.

Every month, we make a difference

Every month we use the collective intelligence of the community to award funds to the most endorsed projects.

Your money only goes to projects that you endorse directly or through people or groups you endorse.

Struggling new ideas for change get a big boost and ambitious projects can do so much more. Learn more
