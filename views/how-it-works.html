How it works

  1. Join OneCommons by committing to a fixed monthly donation (as little as a penny a day!).
  2. You choose which projects and organizations to support/sponsor/endorse. Since you're not donating a specific amount of money, you can choose as many as you like. Or you can just follow the endorsements of people you trust.
  3. Every month we use the collective intelligence of the community to figure out how to maximize the impact of your monthly contribution to the projects and organizations you've endorsed. Learn more
  4. The more you engage and contribute the more your endorsements count. Learn more
